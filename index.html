Ingredients for (8 pices)

Cake Base

  • 15 digestive biscuits
  • 75 g butter (or margarine)

Filling

  • 4 gelatin sheets
  • 2 eggs
  • 400 g cream cheese
  • 2 dl crème fraîche
  • 1.5 dl granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla sugar
  • 1 lemon, grated zest and juice

Glaze

  • 1 gelatin sheet
  • 1 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 4 tbsp water
  • 4 passion fruits

Instructions

Base

  1. Crush the biscuits finely.
  2. Melt the butter.
  3. Mix together and then press the crumbs into a round form with a removable bottom.
  4. Place in the refrigerator.

Filling

  1. Place 4 gelatin sheets in cold water for about 5 minutes.
  2. Separate the eggs into yolks and whites.
  3. Mix the yolks, cream cheese, crème fraîche, sugar, vanilla sugar, and lemon zest into a smooth batter.
  4. Whip the egg whites to stiff peaks and fold them into the batter.
  5. Heat the lemon juice in a saucepan and then remove from the heat.
  6. Squeeze the water out of the gelatin sheets and let them melt in the lemon juice.
  7. Let the mixture cool slightly before gently stirring it into the cheese batter.
  8. Pour the mixture over the cake base and place it back in the refrigerator for at least one hour.

Glaze

  1. Soak the gelatin in cold water for about 5 minutes.
  2. Heat the sugar and water in a saucepan.
  3. Squeeze the water out of the gelatin sheet and let it melt in the sugar solution.
  4. Scrape the passion fruit into the sugar solution and stir.
  5. Let it cool.
  6. Carefully pour the glaze over the filling on the cake.
  7. Let it set in the refrigerator for at least one hour.
